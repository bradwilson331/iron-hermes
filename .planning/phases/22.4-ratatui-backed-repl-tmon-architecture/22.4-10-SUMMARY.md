### Frame 5 — `snap_scroll_active_indicator`  *(WARNING-05 gate)*
- Transcript shows 17 alternating You/Hermes rows (transcript chunk is 18 rows minus 2 border rows of which 1 is title)
- **Border title:** `Chat [live]` (NOT `Chat [scroll N/M]`)

  **Observation:** After `reconcile_scroll(Rect::new(0, 0, 80, 19))` on 30 messages × 1 row each = 30 lines, the max-scroll on a 19-row viewport (17 content rows) is 30 − 17 = 13. Because we asked for `transcript_scroll = 20` which exceeds max=13, `reconcile_scroll` clamped us to max AND re-enabled `auto_follow` per the `else if self.transcript_scroll >= max` branch. Result: indicator goes back to `[live]` and content is bottom-anchored.

  This is a **deterministic, contract-correct** state — the frame locks "what happens when scroll state is over-shot and reconciled" rather than an artificial `scroll 13/13`. If a future regression makes `reconcile_scroll` no-op or off-by-one, this snapshot will diff.

  **Follow-up note for operator:** if the intent was to lock a visible `[scroll N/M]` indicator, a follow-up plan could seed `transcript_scroll = 5` (within-range) and keep `auto_follow = false`. This plan's choice preserves the stricter clamp-plus-reconcile contract.
- Knight-rider row: blank (no `pending_rx`)
- Status row: default pills
