- `App::handle_mouse` (app.rs:299-313) only matches `MouseEventKind::ScrollUp` and `ScrollDown`. All other kinds — `Down(_)`, `Up(_)`, `Drag(_)`, `Moved` — fall through `_ => {}` and are silently discarded. So even with mouse capture on, clicks have no observable effect.
