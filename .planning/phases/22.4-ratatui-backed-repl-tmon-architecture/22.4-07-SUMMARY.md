## ¬ßAgentLoop Integration

**Approach chosen: 3 ‚Äî duplicate AgentLoop builder in spawn_turn** (plan ¬ßAgentLoop Integration option 3).

- `run_agent_turn` at main.rs:1657 takes 16 args including `Arc<TuiHandle>` (Phase 21 infra, retired by D-09) ‚Äî incompatible with tui_rata
- `AgentLoop` does not expose public `client()` or `registry()` getters
- `AgentLoop::run(&mut self, messages)` is the public method, but `Arc<AgentLoop>` prevents `&mut` access without locking
- **Solution:** `App` stores `client: AnyClient` + `registry: Arc<RwLock<ToolRegistry>>` + `budget`, `context_length`, etc. (added to `AppDeps` via Rule 2). `spawn_turn` clones these and builds a fresh `AgentLoop` per turn with a `StreamCallback` that sends `StreamEvent::Delta` chunks
