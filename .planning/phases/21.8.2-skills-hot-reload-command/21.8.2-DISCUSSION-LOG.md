## Claude's Discretion

- **Hot-swap mechanism:** `Arc<RwLock<SkillRegistry>>` vs `ArcSwap` vs threading a fresh Arc — planner picks fewest call-site changes.
- **CommandResult variant for reload:** New `CommandResult::SkillsReload(diff)` vs synchronous inline swap returning `CommandResult::Output(diff_text)` — McpReload is the reference pattern.
- **SKILL-13 CommandResult variant:** New `CommandResult::SkillActivated { name, body }` vs immediate fold into CommandContext for next turn — planner picks consistent with `/personality` overlay pattern.

## Deferred Ideas

- Disk-side rename of Title Case directories (`hermes skills fix-names` command — future phase)
