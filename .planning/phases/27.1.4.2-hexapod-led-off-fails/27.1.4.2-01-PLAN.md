<objective>
Fix two bugs in `ironhermes-tools` that affect physical-robot behavior and test reliability:

1. **Bug 1 (PROV-HEXAPOD wire protocol):** `CMD_LED_OFF` is `"CMD_LED#0\n"` (wrong channel). Freenove's `CMD_LED` is the *color* channel; the off path is on the *mode* channel: `"CMD_LED_MOD#0\n"`. Symptom: `led_off` action does not turn the LEDs off on the physical robot.

2. **Bug 2 (cross-module ENV_LOCK race):** `hexapod_tcp::tests` and `hexapod_video::tests` each declare their own module-local `static ENV_LOCK`. They race on `HEXAPOD_IP` env var because the locks are separate statics. Symptom: `test_capture_frame_passes_allowlist` intermittently sees a `set_var("HEXAPOD_IP", "127.0.0.255")` from a concurrent `hexapod_tcp` test and emits the wrong error.

Purpose: Restore correct robot behavior (LEDs actually turn off) and remove a known test-suite race, with a static-grep invariant locking the fix so a future refactor cannot silently revert.

Output: corrected constant + repurposed test, crate-level ENV_LOCK, swapped imports in both modules, new invariant file.
</objective>
