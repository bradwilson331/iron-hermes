## Key Design Confirmations

- **Unit variant in both enums:** `ResetTerminal` carries no payload in either the core or TUI enum. No `String` field. Per UI-SPEC CLR-7 ("After the visual reset, the REPL issues the next `You: ` prompt. No intermediate text is printed").
