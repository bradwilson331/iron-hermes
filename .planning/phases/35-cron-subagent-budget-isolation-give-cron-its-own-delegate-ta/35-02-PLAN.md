<objective>
Retire the PROV-10 shared parent↔child iteration counter GLOBALLY (D-01, D-02, D-04).
At the single change site `subagent_runner.rs:283-284`, replace
`agent = agent.with_budget(budget.clone())` (the parent's SHARED `BudgetHandle`)
with a FRESH per-child `agent = agent.with_budget(BudgetHandle::new(max_iterations))`.
`run_child` already receives `max_iterations: usize` (param at ~:198, threaded from
delegate_task after Plan 35-01's clamp), so this is a one-line wiring swap — no new
config, no signature change to `run_child`. Every subagent (interactive AND cron) now
gets its own independent budget; a child can no longer decrement its parent's counter.

Purpose: Matches the hermes-agent reference (each subagent owns its budget; total tree
iterations may exceed any single agent's cap, bounded instead by
`max_spawn_depth × max_concurrent_children × max_iterations`). Resolves T-28.1-16 as a
consequence: with no shared parent/child counter, the shared `ToolRegistry` delegate
runner is no longer a contamination vector (proven by Plan 35-03's cron test).

Output: The runner swap, the rewritten PROV-10 doc-comments, the inverted/repurposed
shared-counter assertions, and the new D-07.1 independence unit test.

Field-disposition decision (Claude's Discretion D-disc, RESOLVED HERE): KEEP the
`AgentSubagentRunner.budget` field and the `Option<BudgetHandle>` param on
`AgentSubagentRunner::new` — do NOT remove them. Rationale: RESEARCH recommended removal
assuming only one consumer, but live-code audit found the `new(` constructor is consumed
at TWO production sites (`agent_runtime.rs:149` passing `Some(budget.clone())` and
`tui_rata/event_loop.rs:243` passing `None`) AND grep-locked by two invariant tests
(`invariants_21_7.rs:25-37` requires exactly-1 site in agent_runtime.rs;
`invariants_22_4.rs:567` asserts event_loop.rs contains the call). Removing the param is
therefore more invasive than RESEARCH estimated and would break those grep invariants.
Keeping the field unused-by-children is explicitly the planner's blessed choice. After the
swap the field simply stops being read at 283-284; mark it so the crate still compiles
without dead-code errors (the field is still WRITTEN by `new` and stored on Self).
</objective>
