<decisions_made>

- Server-side `Message::Ping` every 5s (not client-side, not custom reconnect backoff) — browsers auto-pong at the WS protocol level, so D-10 (`with_automatic_reconnect()` only) and D-11 (scoped transient-state reset) stay intact and no `warp_hermes.rs` change was needed.
- Failed keepalive Ping classified as send-path failure per D-05: abort in-flight turn (if any), emit `send_close_frame(CloseCode::Away, "keepalive failed")`, break the `tokio::select!` loop.

- `send_close_frame` errors silently swallowed — a half-broken transport may already reject the close frame and teardown must not block on its delivery (respects D-06's intent not to hang on broken-send paths).
- Regression anchors in `tests/websocket_lifecycle_parity.rs` now lock both the teardown-close-frame invariant and the keepalive invariant (constant name, 5s cadence, `MissedTickBehavior::Skip`, first-tick consumption, Ping emission, `_ = keepalive.tick() =>` select integration, `"keepalive failed"` reason string).
